Curry

Description

Curry is a sauce seasoned with spices, originally associated with South Asian cuisine. It can be dry, or a sauce, and can include any types of meat or fish.

Thought to be orinally from India, many counties have created their own version of the dish, for example Japanese curry.

ingredients

  • Spices
  • Meat and/or fish of your choice
  • Vegetables
  • Cocunut milk or other stock

Steps

  1. Stir fry the meat or vegetables in the spice mix
  2. Pour in the cocunut milk or stock and simmer
  3. Serve with rice, or a freshly-made bread of your choice
